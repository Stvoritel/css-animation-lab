  1. Click "Start AR" button - This will request camera access
  2. Allow camera access when your browser asks for permission
  3. Point your camera at a flat surface (floor or table)
  4. Tap on the screen to place your quantum avatar
  5. Use gestures:
    • ðŸ“ Pinch to zoom in/out
    • ðŸ”„ Drag with one finger to rotate avatar

Mobile Tips:

  • Hold phone horizontally for best experience
  • Good lighting improves tracking
  • Move slowly for stable AR
  • Works best on Chrome or Safari

Troubleshooting:

  • AR requires HTTPS connection
